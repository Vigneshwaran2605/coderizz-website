VISION

To create a club that empowers individuals and excels them in programming,regardless of their background or experience.We aim to create an inclusive and diverse community of coders where individuals can share their knowledge and learn from one another. To empower students to develop their programming skills, collaborate with peers, and build innovative solutions to real-world problems and provide a learning environment that inspires creativity,curiosity, and critical thinking.

MISSION

We aim to foster a love for programming and to equip our members with the current skills and knowledge. To provide high-quality cutting-edge coding resources and education to anyone who has an interest in programming and offer a wide range of coding resources, workshops,and events that cater to the diverse needs and goals of our members. To learn from each other and work together on coding projects that have a positive impact on society.
